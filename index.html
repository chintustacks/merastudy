Online Events

Online Techfest is one of the the unique activity organized by Merastudy . Merastudy organizes Online Technical Fests, two times in a year: 1ST (organized in Even Semester) and 2ND(organized in Odd Semester).To Explore the skills.Students from all the Colleges of BIHAR participate in these Online Techfests. To participate in these Events, there is no need to visit any particular place, students can participate from anywhere using Internet enabled Computer / Smartphone.
